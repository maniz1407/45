Unnbadhil Vendi is a song from the Tamil film Taramani, released in 2017. The song is composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja, and the lyrics are written by Na. Muthukumar. The vocals for this track are provided by Yuvan Shankar Raja himself. Unnbadhil Vendi is a soulful and emotional song that reflects the theme of love and its complexities. The lyrics delve into the intricacies of relationships, capturing the highs and lows that come with love. The music, coupled with poignant lyrics, adds depth to the overall emotional impact of the song. The film Taramani, directed by Ram, is known for its realistic portrayal of contemporary relationships and urban life
